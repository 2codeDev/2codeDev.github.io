Privacy Policy for Funny Little Paperclip Extension

Effective Date: September 3rd, 2025

1. Introduction

This extension, featuring a funny little paperclip, is designed solely for entertainment purposes. It adds a small, humorous character to your browser to provide tips, jokes, or animations while you browse the web.

Your privacy is important. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information from users.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, address)
  • Health information
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Personal communications (emails, chat messages, etc.)
  • Location data (IP, GPS, or region)
  • Web history or visited websites
  • User activity such as clicks, scrolls, or mouse movements
  • Website content

In short, your data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

3. Permissions

The extension requests the following permission(s):

  • activeTab – This allows the extension to display the paperclip on the current tab for entertainment purposes only. It does not read, store, or transmit your data.

No other permissions are used, and there is no remote code involved. All functionality is contained within the extension package.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third parties. Data is never used for purposes unrelated to the extension’s single purpose of entertainment.

5. Security

Since the extension does not collect or store any data, there are no associated data security risks.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy on this page.
